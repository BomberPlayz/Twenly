Title HTML class

You can give a cool-looking title to nearly every component with the help of Twenly's title class.

Tip! You can give titles for native HTML elements too!
Simple usage
Card with simple header
Some content.
Card with big title
And here is some quality content!

<div class="ui card">
    <div class="content">
        <div class="ui header">Card with simple header</div>
    </div>
    <div class="content">Some content.</div>
</div>

<!-- Card with title -->
<div class="ui card">
    <div class="title big">Card with big title</div> <!-- Remove big class if regular title needed -->
    <div class="content">And here is some quality content!</div>
</div>
