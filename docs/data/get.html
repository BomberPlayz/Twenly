Use Twenly in your website

It is very easy to integrate Twenly into your website!

Just copy and paste the following snippet into the head element of your site!

<link rel="stylesheet" href="http://84.236.103.254:1234/Twenly/main.css">
<script src="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/npm/jquery@3.3.1/dist/jquery.min.js"></script>
<script src="http://84.236.103.254:1234/Twenly/main.js"></script>

After you have pasted the snipped, you're good to go!
