Cards

With cards you can easily arrange stuff, create panels, and a whole lot of other things!

Cards can have headers, and footers, everything in content blocks.

Cards with titles don't need content blocks for their title!

Card example
Card with header
Some great content.

Card with title instead of header.
Titles don't need content blocks!

Card with header and footer
Some great content.
Hello!
Some great footer text 

<div class="ui card">
    <div class="content">
        <div class="ui header">Card with header</div>
    </div>
    <div class="content">Some great content.</div>
    </div>
    <br>
<div class="ui card">
    <div class="title">
        Card with title instead of header.
    </div>
    <div class="content">Titles don't need content blocks!</div>
</div>
    <br>
<div class="ui card">
    <div class="content">
        <div class="ui header">Card with header and footer</div>
    </div>
    <div class="content">Some great content.<br>Hello!</div>
    <div class="content">
        <div class="footer">
            Some great footer text
        </div>
    </div>
</div>

As you can see, creating a card is very easy. You can put any content in content blocks.
Content blocks seperate content from other content blocks.
